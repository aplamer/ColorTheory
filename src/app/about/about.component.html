What is color theory?

Color theory is a set of priniciples and guidelines that help artists and designers utilize colors in order to serve a specific purpose within their work.

While color theory is a complicated and very complex concept it can be generally broken down into 3 pieces: Color, Color Harmony and Symbolism. While on their own these three parts of color theory are somewhat easy to grasp it's the use and combination of all three in conjunction with one another that makes color theory so multifaceted.
